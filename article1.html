Tiny Bear Amigurumi Pattern

Photo and Pattern by @katerina_gushchina_ 

        
Bahan-bahan:
Benang Alize Bella, 50 gram/180 m (100%) katun
Hakpen 1,5 mm 
Mata Boneka 2 mm 
benang sulam hitan 
krayon pink 
jarum jahit dan gunting
dakron/silikon 
        
Singkatan:
R = baris/row
mc = magic circle
ch = chain
sc = single crochet
inc = 2 sc di stitch berikutnya
dec = single crochet 2 stitches
F.o. = finish off, tutup
(x) = jumlah stitches
( )*y = mengulang perintah dalam kurung sebanyak y kali

Kepala 
R 1: 6 sc in mc (6)
R 2: inc*6 (12)
R 3: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
R 4: (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
R 5: (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
R 6: (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
R 7-12: 36 sc (6 rows)
R 13: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
R 14: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
R 15: (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
R 16: (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
R 17: dec*6 (6)
F.o.

Kaki (buat 2)
R 1: 6 sc in mc (6)
R 2: inc*6 (12)
R 3-5: 12 sc (3 rows)
jangan potong benang ketika selesai membuat kaki kedua!!!

R 6: From second leg: 2 ch, join with the first leg, 12 sc on the firs leg, 2 ch, 12 sc on the second leg (28)
 
lanjut merajut badannya:
R 7-11: 28 sc (5 rows)
(1 sc, dec)*repeat to the end, closing the hole.
F.o. and leave a long tail for sewing.

jahit lubang di antara kaki

Arms (buat 2)
R 1: 6 sc in mc (6)
R 2-6: 6 sc (5 rows)
F.o. dan sisakan benang yang panjang untuk menjahit nanti.

Ears (make 2)
R 1: 6 sc in mc (6)
R 2: 6 sc
F.o. and leave a long tail for sewing.

Pita
Make 4 ch.
R 1: From the 2nd ch from hook: 3 sc, 1 turning chain
R 2-5: 3 sc, 1 turning chain
R 6: 3 sc
F.o.

Wrap the middle of the bow with yarn a couple of times and sew to the head.

Sulam mulut, hidung, dan alis.
beri pemerah pipi memakai krayon.
